Meet the Graphic Designer

Miguel Cabisada, a Multimedia Arts student from Cavite, is an emerging graphic designer with a passion for visual communication. With his strong foundation in design principles and his creative mindset, Miguel specializes in creating stunning graphics, branding, and digital art that resonate with audiences.

Miguel is known for his ability to combine aesthetics with functionality. He has worked on various design projects, including logo creation, advertising materials, and digital illustrations. His works reflect a unique style that emphasizes clarity, innovation, and emotion in every design.
